Ayran Recipe

Ayran is a traditional yoghurt drink from Turkiye!..

Ingredients:

  • Yoghurt (Turk)
  • Water
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Add as much yoghurt as you want into a mixing cup.
  2. Whisk yoghurt until it starts to thin
  3. Slowly add water while whisking (Add 3 parts water for 2 parts yoghurt.)
  4. Add 1 teaspoon of salt per 3 glasses of ayran.
  5. Shake it well in a shaker if you can, Afiyet Olsun!
