Karnıyarık Recipe

Karnıyarık is an eggplant dish from Turkiye.

Ingredients:

  • Eggplant
  • Ground Beef
  • Onions and Garlic
  • Green Pepper
  • Tomatoes
  • Tomato Paste
  • Salt, Pepper and/or any other spice..

Instructions:

  1. Peel the eggplants (Leave some stripes)
  2. Cut a slit in the middle of eggplants and place the eggplants inside salty water to get rid of bitterness. (approx. 30 min.)
  3. Prepare the filling. Saute Onion -> Garlic -> Ground Beef -> Pepper, Tomatoes and Tomato Paste, Salt Pepper.
  4. Fry the eggplants in oil untill brown.
  5. Fill the eggplants, put them on a tray with some water and bake 30 mins. Afiyet olsun.
